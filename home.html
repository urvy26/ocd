FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is OCD? Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD) is a mental health disorder characterized by intrusive thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors or mental acts (compulsions).
How common is OCD? OCD affects about 2.3% of adults in the United States at some point in their lives.
What are common symptoms of OCD? Common symptoms of OCD include excessive hand washing, checking things repeatedly, counting, and having unwanted intrusive thoughts.
